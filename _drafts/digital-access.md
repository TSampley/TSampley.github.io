At one time in the past, you could search available degrees programs on a university's website, including any requirement sheets and special or accelerated programs, which is valuable because it allows prospective students to compare the rigor and intensity of various degrees across colleges, whether their priorities are an easygoing, minimal program just to get that piece of paper or the most prestigious and rigorous program in the world.
https://anthro.ucla.edu/academics/undergraduate/bachelor-of-science/
https://www.sscnet.ucla.edu/anthro/forms/bs_checklist.pdf

For a variety of reasons, universities are increasingly using digital materials in their courses. Not only does the access expire at the end of the semester, some of the most egregious examples shared online, show interstitial ads playing between pages.
