Pride is a disease, and we are in a pandemic. The U.S. is having an especially bad time with it.

Pride can be useful in an evolutionary or strategic sense. Many people with a strong sense of pride can fake their way through an interview with a strong sense of confidence even if they don't have the required skills because they truly believe they deserve the job.
Humility is important to the individual to avoid taking on a burden they can't handle.
Humility is important to the group to avoid expecting too much and over-burdening the individual or to avoid giving a critical task to someone with a high expectation of failure.
