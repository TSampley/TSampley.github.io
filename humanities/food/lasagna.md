## Info
- Serves: 12
- Preparation: 30min
- Cooking: 45min
- Total: 1hr 15min

## Ingredients

### Ricotta Filling
- 15 oz ricotta cheese
- 1 large egg
- 2 C mozzarella cheese
- 3/4 C parmesan
- 2 t italian seasoning
- 1/2 t salt
- 1/4 t pepper
{: .recipe-ingredient-list}

### Meat Filling
- 1 T olive oil
- 1 yellow onion, finely diced
- 3/4 lb ground beef
- 3/4 lb ground, italian sausage
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 C chicken broth
- 40 oz marinara sauce
- 1 T tomato paste
- 1 t hot sauce
- 1 t worcestire sauce
{: .recipe-ingredient-list}

### Bones
- 12 lasagna noodles
- 2 1/2 C mozzarella cheese
{: .recipe-ingredient-list}

## Instructions

1. Combine all ricotta filling ingredients and set aside.
2. Heat the olive oil in a pan.
3. Add diced onion and let soften ~10 minutes.
4. Add ground meats and increase heat to medium for 8-10 minutes.
5. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute; drain excess grease.
6. Add chicken broth and scrape sides and bottom of pot.
7. Add remaining meat filling ingredients and bring to a simmer; cover partially while boiling noodles.
8. Cook lasagna noodles in large pot of water till al dente; drain and rinse.
9. Spread 1 C meat sauce as a base, then repeat three times: noodles (4), 1/3 cheese mix, 1 1/2 C meat sauce
10. Top with mozzarella
11. Bake @375ÂºF for 35 minutes covered then 10 minutes uncovered.
12. Allow to cool before serving.
