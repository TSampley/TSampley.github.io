Reasoning is best understood as a biological adaptation, rather than 
the high-minded philosophical activity it might have a reputation for. [Logic](../../philosophy/logic/) is worthy of higher regard, but reasoning gets conflated with it, only because the best reasoning is structured with logic; however, even without a scientific or rigorous understanding of the world around it, organisms benefit tremendously from being able to build causal models of their environment.
